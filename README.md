# SV08-Modular-Top-Hat
A top hat with modular panels

Print 4 Foundation Left, 4 Foundation Right, 2 Foundation Corners, 2 Foundation Corner Mirrored, 2 Panel Corners, 2 Panel Corners Reversed, and your choice of modular panels. 

Filter Panels use 8025 fans and Shark AI AV2501 HEPA Filters - https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0B6P7HV23 . Space to cut and insert carbon filters behind the HEPA filter.

Umbilical Panel currently mounts a Fsytec Stridemax and an Orbitool O2/O2S Toolboard. More variants will come. Stridemax can be cooled with a 6010 fan. Print a PUG of your choice (included is a 8mm ID PUG produced by this fantastic parametric remix https://www.printables.com/model/1334911-pug-parametric-umbilical-gland-remixed-incl-fusion) and this PUG mount - https://www.printables.com/model/767060-voron-24-umbilical-exhaust-grill-for-pug
